The App: FluentFast

A multiplayer language learning app.

Encouraging, fun, and built to play with friends.

Aiming to create a rich, immersive language learning environment through peer-to-peer learning.

Immersion is the best form of learning... but what if you can't travel?

This app aims to bring the immersive experience to you.

When learning with friends, you also practice with friends... Learning fluent faster together.

Able to learn off the app also, with friends. Maybe with family.

We have multiple paths we can execute, we just need to know which one you want the most.
