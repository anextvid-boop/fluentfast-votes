What is Fluent Fast?

We are creating the world's most immersive, peer-to-peer language learning format. Traditional apps use solitary mini-games—but language is meant to be spoken! Fluent Fast drops you into collaborative real-time multiplayer scenarios where you talk, learn, and grow alongside real people.
