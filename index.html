What is Fluent Fast?

We are creating the world's most immersive, peer-to-peer language learning format. Traditional apps use solitary mini-games. We believe language is meant to be spoken. Fluent Fast drops you into collaborative, real-time multiplayer scenarios where you are forced to talk, learn, and grow alongside real people.
